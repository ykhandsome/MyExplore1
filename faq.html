Account Management

How do I create an account?

On the top right corner of the page, you'll find the "Sign In" button. Click on it. At the bottom of this new page, you'll find the "Create account" option. Click on it. You can choose to sign up using an existing Google, Facebook, or Apple ID account. Alternatively, you can sign up with your email. Fill in your First Name, Last Name, and Email, then create a password. After filling in the information, click on "Sign Up". You'll receive a verification email in your inbox. Click on the link in the email to verify your account.

How do I sign in with Google?

When you choose to sign in with your Google Account, it links to the email associated with the account. Click on the "Sign In" button at the top right corner of the page. A new page will open. Click on the "Google" button under the "or sign in with" section. This will prompt a new window where you can choose the Google account you'd like to sign in with. Choose the account you want to use or sign in to another Google account if your account is not on the list. After selecting the Google Account, you may need to enter your password depending on your Google Account settings. Once you enter the password, the window will close and you will be signed in to your account.

How do I sign in with Apple ID?

Click on the "Sign In" button at the top right corner of the page. A new page will open. There, click on the "Apple" button under the "or sign in with" section. A pop-up window will ask for your Apple ID. Please enter your Apple ID and password. If you're using Safari on an Apple device and you're signed in to your Apple ID, you might not need to enter your Apple ID or password. After verifying your Apple ID, Apple will ask if you want to share your email. You can choose to share your email or hide it. Once validated, the window will close, and you will be signed in to your account.

How do I sign in with a Facebook Account?

Click on the "Sign In" button at the top right corner of the page. A new page will open. There, click on the "Facebook" button under the "or sign in with" section. You will be redirected to a Facebook login page. Please enter your Facebook associated email address or phone number, and password. Click on "Log In".

How do I change my Password?

Log in to your account. Hover over your profile picture situated at the top right corner of the page and click on "Profile". Once on the "Profile" page, select "Edit" next to the Password Field. You will need to enter your current password and then enter your new password. Confirm the new password by retyping it. After you've entered all the information, click on "Save" to change your password.

How do I delete my account?

Log in to your account. Hover over your profile picture situated at the top right corner of the page and click on "Profile". Once on the "Profile" page, scroll all the way down and select "Delete my account".

Cancel Reservation

How can I cancel my reservation?

You can cancel your reservation online. Any cancellation fees are determined by the property and are set out in the cancellation policy of the relevant booking.

Do I need to pay if I cancel my reservation?

If you have a reservation that allows free cancellation, you don't have to pay a cancellation fee. If your reservation is no longer free to cancel or non-refundable, you may be subject to a cancellation fee. Any cancellation fees for reservations are determined by the property.

How will I know that my reservation has been cancelled?

After you cancel your reservation with us, you will receive an email confirming the cancellation. Please check your email inbox and spam inbox.

Where can I find the cancellation policy?

When searching for a room, you should be able to find the booking terms and cancellation policy, as well as other room information. You can also find this information on your booking voucher.

Booking Confirmation

When will I receive the booking confirmation email?

In most cases, you will receive this email within 30 minutes of booking, along with your check-in voucher in PDF format. If you have not received the email after this period, please check your junk/spam inbox. You can also download or resend your check-in voucher online.

Can you resend me my check-in voucher?

A self-service option is now available to resend your voucher by clicking on the self-service link in your booking confirmation email.

I haven't received my booking confirmation yet and can't find the order online

In most cases, you will receive your booking confirmation (PDF file) via email within 30 minutes of completing your booking. If you haven't received the email after this period, please check your spam and/or spam folders. You can also log in and select the "My Bookings" section from the personal account options to view your booking details and status online. If you still can't find your reservation and haven't received confirmation 24 hours after booking, please feel free to contact us.
