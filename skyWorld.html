Welcome to SkyWorlds Theme Park

SkyWorlds Theme Park, located in Genting Highlands, Malaysia, is a world-class outdoor amusement park offering a mix of exciting rides and immersive attractions. The park features themed zones inspired by popular movie franchises, providing visitors with a thrilling blend of adventure, entertainment, and family-friendly fun. With a variety of rides, from high-speed roller coasters to interactive 4D experiences, SkyWorlds caters to visitors of all ages. Set in the cool, scenic highlands, the park offers breathtaking views and a memorable experience, making it a must-visit destination for thrill-seekers and families alike.

Tip: The park is less crowded on weekdays, which means shorter wait times for rides. Get to the park when it opens to take advantage of quieter times and avoid long lines for popular attractions.

What to Bring

  • Backpack: A small backpack is useful for carrying essentials like water, snacks, and extra clothing.
  • Portable Charger: You’ll likely use your phone for photos or checking the park map, so having a portable charger ensures you stay connected.
  • Cashless Payment: The park may use a cashless system, so bring a card or use mobile payment for food and merchandise.

Dining Options

SkyWorlds has a variety of food stalls and restaurants. Try themed dining experiences to enhance your visit.

Other Attractions

Explore Genting Highlands: Make time to explore other attractions in the area, such as the casino, shopping outlets, or indoor theme parks.
