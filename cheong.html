Welcome to Cheong Fatt Tze Blue Mansion

Cheong Fatt Tze Blue Mansion, located in George Town, Penang, is an iconic heritage building and a masterpiece of Sino-eclectic architecture. Built in the late 19th century by Cheong Fatt Tze, a prominent Chinese merchant and philanthropist, the mansion is renowned for its striking indigo-blue walls, intricate carvings, and antique furnishings. The mansion blends Eastern and Western architectural elements, symbolizing the fusion of cultures in Penang. Today, it serves as a boutique hotel and museum, offering visitors a glimpse into the opulent lifestyle of Penang's elite during the colonial era.

The mansion offers guided tours three times a day, typically at 11:00 AM, 2:00 PM, and 3:30 PM. These tours are the best way to fully appreciate the history, architecture, and cultural significance of the mansion. Arrive early to secure your spot, especially during peak tourist seasons.

Transport Recommendations

  • By Car or Taxi: The Blue Mansion is located in the heart of George Town, Penang, making it easily accessible by car or taxi. It's about a 30-minute drive from Penang International Airport.
  • By Public Transport: You can take the Rapid Penang bus to the Chulia Street bus stop, which is a short walk from the mansion.

Cultural Sensitivity

The Blue Mansion is a UNESCO World Heritage site and a cultural landmark, so be respectful of the space. Refrain from touching artifacts or furniture, and follow all guidelines provided by your tour guide.

Dining Recommendations

The mansion houses a fine dining restaurant, Indigo, which offers a blend of Asian and European cuisine. Reservations are recommended, especially for dinner.
