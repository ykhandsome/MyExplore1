Welcome to Splash Mania Waterpark

SplashMania Waterpark is a premier water park located in Gamuda Cove, Malaysia. It offers a variety of thrilling water attractions, catering to both adrenaline-seekers and families. The park boasts over 70 water slides and attractions, including wave pools, lazy rivers, and high-speed rides. The waterpark is designed to provide a fun and adventurous experience with state-of-the-art facilities, making it an ideal destination for a day of excitement and relaxation. SplashMania also emphasizes safety and comfort, ensuring a memorable visit for all ages.

Tip: To avoid crowds, plan your trip on weekdays, especially in the mornings.

What to Bring

  • Waterproof Phone Case: If you plan to take photos, a waterproof case will protect your phone.
  • Cashless Payment: Bring a card or mobile payment method, as many parks operate cashless systems for convenience.

Safety

  • Follow Park Rules: Observe the safety instructions for each ride to ensure a safe experience.
  • Supervise Children: Keep an eye on younger kids, especially in wave pools or fast-paced areas.
  • Lifeguards: Take note of where the lifeguards are stationed in case of emergencies.

Protect the Environment

Be mindful of the park's fragile ecosystem. Avoid leaving any waste behind and follow all park guidelines!

Do not disturb the animals or their habitats. Keep a safe distance when observing wildlife!
